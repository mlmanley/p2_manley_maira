The Let It Be Team

Our Story

Our bakery started as a family hobby. After years trying new recipes and several requests from neighbors, we decided it was time to turn our beloved hobby into a business. Let it bakery was founded in 2015 with the goal to continue to create and discover new ways of making artisan breads every day. All our breads are still made in our own kitchen at home, with no preservatives or chemicals, and with all the love and attention that only homemade recipes can offer.

Let it Be offers sweet and savory options, and traditional recipes of Brazilian cuisine. Available for pick up or delivery.
